ABOUT ME.

Ire first trained to become a hair stylist in South Korea in 2009. After a few years of experience, she moved to Australia to learn more about global trends and hairdressing skills. In 2016, she moved to Canada and is now working at Hair Creation in Richmond, BC as a master hairstylist with 11 years of training and experience.


Ire achieved her “Colour Diploma” from Lóreal Professional and her certificate in cut and colour from Kevin Murphy. She also specializes in Asian balayage ombre services.
